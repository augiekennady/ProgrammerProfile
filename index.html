About Me

I worked in the film industry for six years prior to attending Columbia University. Once in school, I focused on mathematics and film studies while dabbling in a myriad of other subjects.

Upon graduation, I found it exceedingly difficult to get work, so I began tutoring! I tutored in many fields of mathematics, as well as economics, astronomy, physics, German, Japanese, film production, film editing, writing, and college application essays.

This led to a job in customer support at Chegg, and I worked my way up from customer support to be the manager of business operations, which is what I do now!

To support my lavish spending habits and thirst for new knowledge, I also have maintained side employment positions related to marketing e-commerce companies.

I'm now working as a consultant in software development so I can learn more about e-commerce trends and hopefully help shape the future of both e-commerce and online education!
